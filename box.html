Programming is the process of designing, writing, testing, and maintaining instructions (called code) that a computer can execute to perform specific tasks or solve problems. It involves using programming languages, such as Python, Java, C++, or JavaScript, to communicate with a computer and create functional software, applications, games, websites, and other digital tools. At its core, programming requires logical thinking and problem-solving skills. Programmers break down complex problems into smaller, manageable steps and create algorithms—step-by-step instructions—that guide the computer on how to solve these problems.
